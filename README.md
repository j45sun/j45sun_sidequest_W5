## Description

> This side quest is an edited version of week 5 example 5 code shown in class.
> The edited version allows the player to move and jump freely through the map, while being on the moon.
> The atmosphere resembles the moon, where the player spawns on the moon and can jump higher as the player is in space. The stars in the background represents space.
> The player can walk or jump to the end of the map and will be greeted by a small alien.

---

## Interaction Instructions

> To move, press A/D or ←/→  
> To jump, press Space/W/↑
